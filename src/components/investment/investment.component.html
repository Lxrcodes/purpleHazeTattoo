The Process & Investment

Investing in a tattoo is investing in a piece of art you'll carry forever. Here is a transparent breakdown of my pricing structure to help you plan your next piece.

This base rate covers smaller tattoos and the cost of a fully sterile, single-use equipment setup, ensuring the highest standards of safety and hygiene for every client.

For larger and multi-session pieces, the hourly rate applies. You are only charged for the time the needle is running. This does not include drawing time, stencil application, or breaks.

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment date and begin the design process. This amount is credited towards the final cost of your tattoo.
